The Story

Learning Out Loud

I'm not a professional musician. I don't have a music degree or years of training. I picked up production as an adult and decided to do it the hard way: no shortcuts, everything from scratch.

The channel exists because the process of figuring things out is worth sharing. Not just the polished result, but the fumbling, the breakthroughs, and the slow grind of getting slightly better each session.

The music lives somewhere between reggae, funk, soul, and whatever feels right in the moment. Genres are a starting point, not a cage.
