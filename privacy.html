Privacy Policy

Effective May 1, 2026

What we collect

If you subscribe to the Tumak newsletter, we collect only the email address you submit. Nothing else. The email goes into our newsletter provider so we can write to you when there's something new.

What we don't collect

We don't track you across the web. We don't run third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or cookies beyond what's needed to make the site load. We don't sell, share, or rent your information to anyone.

Automated posting pipeline

Tumak Music operates an automated content pipeline that posts short-form videos to the creator's own social media accounts (TikTok, X, YouTube) using OAuth tokens generated by the platform owner. The pipeline only authorizes posting to those owned accounts. No third-party user data is processed, stored, or shared by this system.

Your data, your control

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter anytime via the link in every email we send. If you'd like your email permanently removed from our records, write to tumak@tumakmusic.com and it will be deleted within 7 days.

Updates

If this policy changes meaningfully, we'll update the date at the top and note what changed.

Contact

Privacy questions? Email tumak@tumakmusic.com.
